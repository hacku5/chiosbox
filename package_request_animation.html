How It Works?

Manage your shipping from Turkey to Europe in four simple steps.

01

Shop

Shop from any e-commerce site in Turkey. Use your ChiosBox address as the delivery address.

02

Arrive at Our Warehouse

Your packages arrive at our secure warehouse on Chios. The barcode is scanned and logged into the system.

03

Photo Approval

Warehouse staff takes a photo of your package. You view it from your panel and verify the contents.

04

Pick Up

Consolidate your packages, save money. Show your QR code, pick up on your island. It's that simple!
